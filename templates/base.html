About Us


Founded in 1998, our corporate office is located in Skokie, Illinois – we have offices in many states in the U.S. such as Indiana, Michigan, Wisconsin, Ohio, New York, Georgia, Kentucky, Tennessee and Florida. Medical Diagnostics Services, Inc. services over 400 long term care facilities, short term rehabilitation facilities, assisted/independent living facilities, group homes and home health care agencies. We also service private physician practices, physician groups, correctional facilities, VA and hospice patients.
